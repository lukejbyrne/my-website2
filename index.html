About

From consulting to creating.

I spent years in enterprise cloud consulting at Accenture and Amdocs, shipping Azure migrations for global clients. In late 2025 I left it all to go full-time as a creator and entrepreneur. Now I run Byrne Media from Carlisle in the North of England, making content, building products, and helping others do the same.

I hold a BSc in Computer Science and an MLitt in Management from the University of St Andrews. When I'm not shipping, I'm a dad, an avid reader, and exploring questions of faith and philosophy.
